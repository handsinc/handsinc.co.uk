Not everyone likes the thought of going to the gym, but put on music and most will start tapping their feet. ‘Move & Relax’is a freestyle movement class where exercise is a fun by-product.

We create a safe non-judgmental space to have fun exploring the natural rhythms of your body. For women of all ages and abilities, no dancing or exercise skills required - just your curiosity and willingness to move.
