Hands Inc has a successful track record of providing wellbeing services within the community, private and public sector. Our business has grown due to recommendation from our service users and clients.

Whether it's your business to look after your own, your staff or your clients health, we have a package that will meet your needs. Our friendly team of therapists and facilitators are all hand picked and professionally qualified so that you can be assured that you are in good hands.

Quality you can rely on

Our PQASSO Quality Assurance mark demonstrates we adopt good governance through our policies, procedures, risk management, monitoring and evaluation systems, meeting standards as commended by the Charity Evaluation Services.

We are members of Embody and The Complementary Therapist Association and are governed by their code of ethics and professional standards.

Our facilitators and therapists are professionally qualified, insured, members of relevant governing bodies and where working with children and vulnerable adults are enhanced DBS checked.
