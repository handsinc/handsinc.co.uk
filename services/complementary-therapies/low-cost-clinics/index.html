Affordable complementary health treatments for the community

Hands Inc is a not-for-profit community organisation based in Hackney. We operate low-cost massage and acupuncture clinics at the John Scott Medical Centre and The Lower Clapton Health Centre in Hackney. If you are a health professional and want to refer clients please contact us and see our Complementary Therapies fact and stats sheet.

Hands Inc provides these clinics with the aim of offering affordable access while empowering individuals to take proactive and sustainable steps towards greater wellbeing and healthier lifestyles. The clinic is run on a self referral basis. Our professionally run clinics are socially inclusive, where health equity is paramount.

People eligible for treatment (proof of eligibility required)

  • Those over 60
  • Those on benefits
  • Refugee or asylum seekers
  • Those with mental health issues
  • Those suffering long term ill health

In order to access the clinic you will need to leave a message on our recorded phone line service and one of our team will get back to you. 0203 051 8626. Pre booking is essential.
