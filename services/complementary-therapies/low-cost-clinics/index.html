Affordable complementary health treatments for the community

Hands Inc is a not-for-profit community organisation based in Hackney. We operate low-cost complementary therapy clinic at Allerton Road Medical Centre in Hackney. If you are a health professional and want to refer clients please contact us.

Hands Inc provides this clinic with the aim of offering affordable access while empowering individuals to take proactive and sustainable steps towards greater wellbeing and healthier lifestyles. Treatments are priced on a sliding scale between £17 & £40. The clinic is run on a self referral basis. Our professionally run clinics are socially inclusive, where health equity is paramount.

Please call us on 0203 051 8626 to discuss making a booking or referral. This is not a walk in clinic and all treatments must be pre-booked.
