Affordable complementary health treatments for the community

Hands Inc operates this low-cost complementary therapy clinic in Hackney to help increase access. Treatments are priced on a sliding scale between £17 & £40 and takes self referrals. Our professionally run clinics are socially inclusive, to promote health equity is paramount and empower individuals to take proactive and sustainable steps towards greater wellbeing and healthier lifestyles.

Please call us on 0203 051 8626 to discuss making a booking or referral. This is not a walk in clinic and all treatments must be pre-booked.
