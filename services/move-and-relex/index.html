Not everyone likes the thought of going to the gym, but put on music and most will start tapping their feet. ‘Move & Relax’ is a women only freestyle movement class where exercise is a fun by-product.

We create a safe non-judgmental space to have fun exploring the natural rhythms of your body. For women of all ages and abilities, no dancing or exercise skills required - just your curiosity and willingness to move.

Suitable for women who shy away from traditional exercise classes, or want a women only space.

When: Tuesdays 10am-12.30
Dates: 3rd June-22nd July
Where: The Edge Centre, 7 Woodberry Grove, Manor House, N4 1SN
Fee: Full course is £20. Can be paid in instalments by arrangement
What to wear: Loose comfortable clothes
