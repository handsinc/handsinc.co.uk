Massage@Work

Offering complementary therapies at work is not just about pampering your staff or massaging peoples egos. It is a proactive way of investing in the health and wellbeing of your organisation and avoiding lost days due to ill health.


Why choose a Massage@Work service:

  • Work-focused treatments ensure that people in work can access investigation of and treatment of health problems in a way that will assist them to remain in work and avoid unnecessary absence and the burden this puts on staff having to pick up the extra work.

  • Our range of complementary therapies can be used as an occupational health package

  • Treatments can be used at promotional and fundraising events to bring attraction to your stand, for corporate days and to reward clients or staff


Treatments Available

We offer a range of stress-busting massage treatments that are oil free and are carried out with the client fully clothed. This makes for a less intrusive treatment suitable for the novice and any setting. Duration of sessions can vary bewteen 10-60 minutes dependent on the occassion and setting. See our Complementary therapy pages to view the wider range of treatments available or contact us to discuss your needs.

Positive Results

  • Boosting the immune system, thereby allowing the body to be more resistant to colds, flu, etc
  • Gives time out to rejuvenate

Benefits to your organisation

Benefits include:

  • No Capital outlay
  • Builds Staff Morale
  • Employees become more energetic, alert, productive and efficient
  • Demonstrates an active commitment to the wellbeing of your staff and adds kudos
